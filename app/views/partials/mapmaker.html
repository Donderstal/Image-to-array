Welcome to the Neckbeard mapmaker tool.

To get started, upload a tilesheet with the button at the right.Then, fill in how many columns and rows you want you map to have, and click the 'Generate grid' button.

Now you are ready to begin making a map!

Click on a tile in the tilesheet, and then click on a tile in the grid on the left top copy it the map. Shift-click a tile in the map to remove it.

When you are happy with your map, click the 'Finish output array' button. This will copy a large string of code to your clipboard. Send it to Daan so he can include your map in the game ASAP!!


After finishing a map, or when you want to discard your current one, refresh the page before starting over.


IMPORTANT

  1. The mapmaker can only work with one tilesheet at a time!
  2. Don't mix tilesheets
